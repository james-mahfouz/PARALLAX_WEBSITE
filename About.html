OpenAI

OpenAI is a research facility for artificial intelligence made up of the for-profit OpenAI LP and the nonprofit OpenAI Inc. Elon Musk, Sam Altman, Greg Brockman, and other prominent tech figures formed it in December 2015 with the goal of safely and profitably advancing AI.

Additional Information

The goal of OpenAI is to create and advance friendly AI for the benefit of all people. The institution undertakes research across a broad spectrum of AI-related domains, including machine learning, computer vision, robotics, and natural language processing. As a result of this research, major advancements have been made in each of these fields.

In addition to conducting research, OpenAI also creates and disseminates tools and technologies that can hasten the advancement of AI, such as the GPT language models, which rank among the biggest and most potent language models at the moment. As part of its ongoing efforts to ensure the ethical and responsible advancement of AI, OpenAI also develops best practices for researchers in the field and fosters cooperation across other AI groups.
