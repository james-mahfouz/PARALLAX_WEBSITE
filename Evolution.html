Premature AI

This ai was in it's early concepts, even the term itself was close to non existant. Most of the work was manual labor but the human mind was working on ways to automate the tasks to remove some of the hard work from humans and put it on machines.

Basic AI

The ai in this era was pretty advanced for it's age, it was used in a lot of systems like filtering systems to prevent spam emails, in virtual assistants for popular websites, in NPCs and enemies for their behaviour in video games, etc.

Marketing AI

Artistic AI

This type of ai can study art from different artists and implement these styles in completely new work of art, but because this ai studies artists and tries to implement their styles it brought a lot of controversy and debate about whether this type of ai is creating or stealing art from the actual artists.
